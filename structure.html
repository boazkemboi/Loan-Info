Group Loan Disbursement Plan

1. Introduction

Our group aims to provide financial support to members through structured loan disbursement while ensuring sustainability. By pooling resources, we create a reliable fund that promotes financial stability and growth among members.

The loan program is designed to offer fair and accessible credit to members, helping them meet personal and business financial needs while fostering a culture of responsible borrowing and timely repayment.

To maintain sustainability, loan eligibility is based on contributions and repayment history. This ensures fair distribution of funds, protects group assets, and supports long-term financial growth for all members.

2. Loan Eligibility Criteria

  • Must have at least 1 month of active contributions.
  • Contribution must be consistent and up to date.
  • The member must not have any outstanding unpaid loans.

3. Loan Limits & Conditions

  • A member can borrow up to 3 times their total contributions.
  • The total loan amount depends on available group funds.
  • Repayment Period & Interest Rates:
    • Up to 30 days: 5% interest rate.
    • 31 to 90 days: 10% interest rate.
    • Above 90 days: 15% interest rate.
  • Emergency Loan Provision:
    • Available for urgent financial needs, such as health emergencies or unforeseen expenses.
    • Processed within 24 hours for quick access.
    • Maximum loan amount: 50% of the member’s total contributions.
    • Flat 3% processing fee applies.
    • Repayment within 60 days at a 7% interest rate.
  • Late Payment Penalty:
    • A fixed KES 50 fine per missed installment.

4. Loan Application Process

  • Members submit a loan request form (physical or online).
  • The loan committee reviews the application based on eligibility.
  • A loan agreement is signed before disbursement.

5. Loan Security & Guarantors

  • Members must provide two guarantors from the group.
  • Guarantors will cover loan repayments if the borrower defaults.
  • A minimum of 20% of the loan amount will be withheld from the borrower’s contributions as collateral.
  • The withheld amount will be released after full loan repayment.

6. Loan Disbursement Process

  • Approved loans are sent via M-Pesa or bank transfer.
  • Loan disbursement is based on the available group funds.
  • Records are maintained for tracking repayments.

7. Loan Recovery & Follow-up

  • Monthly repayment reminders.
  • Deduction from future contributions if default occurs.
  • Dispute resolution and enforcement of penalties.

8. Conclusion

Responsible borrowing and timely repayment sustain the group's financial growth.

An open discussion and Q&A session will address any concerns.
