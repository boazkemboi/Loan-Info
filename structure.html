Group Loan Disbursement Plan

1. Introduction

Overview of the group and its financial goals.

Purpose of the loan program.

Ensuring fair access to loans while maintaining group sustainability.

2. Loan Eligibility Criteria

  • Must have at least 1 month of active contributions.
  • Contribution must be consistent and up to date.
  • The member must not have any outstanding unpaid loans.

3. Loan Limits & Conditions

  • A member can borrow up to 3 times their total contributions.
  • The total loan amount depends on available group funds.
  • Repayment period options:
    • Up to 30 days: 5% interest rate.
    • 31 to 90 days: 10% interest rate.
    • Above 90 days: 15% interest rate.
  • Late payment penalty: A fixed KES 50 fine per missed installment.

4. Loan Application Process

  • Members submit a loan request form (physical or online).
  • The loan committee reviews the application based on eligibility.
  • A loan agreement is signed before disbursement.

5. Loan Security & Guarantors

  • Members must provide two guarantors from the group.
  • Guarantors will cover loan repayments if the borrower defaults.
  • A minimum of 20% of the loan amount will be withheld from the borrowerâ€™s contributions as collateral.
  • The withheld amount will be released after full loan repayment.

6. Loan Disbursement Process

  • Approved loans are sent via M-Pesa or bank transfer.
  • Loan disbursement is based on the available group funds.
  • Records are maintained for tracking repayments.

7. Loan Recovery & Follow-up

  • Monthly repayment reminders.
  • Deduction from future contributions if default occurs.
  • Dispute resolution and enforcement of penalties.

8. Conclusion

Members are encouraged to borrow responsibly and adhere to repayment terms.

Loan repayment allows the group to grow and support more members.

Emphasizing the benefits of timely repayment.

Strengthening group financial sustainability for future borrowing.

Open discussion and Q&A session.
